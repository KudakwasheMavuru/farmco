About Us

Farmco aquaculture limited is an agricultural based company that focuses on growing fruits and vegetables through the use of an advanced agricultural system called hydroponics also known as soilless agriculture

Hydroponics is the method of farming where plants can be grown in nutrient-fortified water, instead of in soil. Given concerns of feeding a growing human population in a changing climate, scientists believe hydroponic technology may be able to mitigate impending food shortages.

Our Features

Why Choose Us

Nutrition-Packed

Hydroponic vegetables are often more nutrient-dense than soil-grown vegetables. This is because their roots can absorb more nutrients from the water because they are not competing for food with other plants.

Pesticide-Free

Pesticides are not required for hydroponically grown vegetables because they are not susceptible to pests and diseases in the same way that soil-grown plants are.

Fresher for Longer

Hydroponic vegetables are typically fresher than soil-grown vegetables because they do not have to travel long distances to reach supermarkets or other retail outlets.
